Quality Policy

Our lifesaving automotive safety systems never get a second chance; hence we are committed to deliver the highest quality, safety, and performance in our products and services, in alignment with our vision of Saving More Lives.

Our quality culture is personified by daily execution in the entire organization to:

  • Reduce risk for critical quality issues and ‘near misses’. Drive toward zero customer issues and zero defects.
  • Provide customers with products and services that are and are perceived more valuable than our competitors’.
  • Reduce waste and internal errors.
  • Increase profitability & growth through application of Zero Defect principles.
